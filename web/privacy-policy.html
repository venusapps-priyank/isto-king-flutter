Privacy Policy

How Venusapps Private Limited handles your information

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Venusapps Private Limited ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you play Chaka (the "Game"), our digital version of the traditional Indian board game. By downloading or using the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Introduction

Chaka is a casual board game designed for local multiplayer fun on a single device. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information associated with your use of the Game. This policy applies to the Chaka application on all supported platforms.

2. Information We Collect

Chaka is built to require as little personal information as possible. In most cases you can play without providing personal details or creating an account.

  • Information you provide: A display name, avatar selection, theme colour, game rules preferences, and settings such as sound, music, vibration, and language. Any information you choose to share if you contact us for support, such as your email address.
  • Information collected automatically: Limited technical data such as device type and model, operating system version, and app version that may be collected by the app store or platform provider for delivery, security, and crash reporting.
  • Game progress data: Your scores, settings, and in-game progress are generally stored on your device during your session.
  • Purchase information: If you make in-app purchases (such as virtual coins or cosmetic items), payment is processed by the app store. We do not receive or store your full payment card or banking details. The store may provide us with a transaction identifier and purchase receipt so we can deliver what you bought.

When using the web version, fonts may be loaded from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of normal web requests. We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information, and we do not ask you for data that is not needed to provide or improve the Game.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Operate, maintain, and provide the features of the Game.
  • Remember your preferences and personalise your gameplay experience.
  • Process and fulfil in-app purchases.
  • Understand how the game is used so we can improve gameplay and performance.
  • Diagnose technical problems, fix bugs, and prevent crashes.
  • Respond to your questions, support requests, and feedback.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security issues.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the Game, such as:

  • App store providers (Google Play, Apple App Store, etc.) for payment processing and purchase verification.
  • Platform and hosting providers (such as app stores, cloud services, and font providers) that support distribution, crash reporting, and storage.

These providers are only permitted to use information as needed to perform services for us. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users or our company.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your device secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Chaka is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a way that violates applicable laws. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at info@venusapps.com so we can take appropriate action to remove it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or delete it, or to object to or restrict certain processing. Because most game data is stored on your device, you can often clear it by deleting the Game or resetting its data from your device settings. For any other requests, contact us at info@venusapps.com and we will respond in accordance with applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the Game or for legal and operational reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Game after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.
